About Us

Founded by a group of passionate DevOps engineers and Machine Learning scientists, Vertex AI Labs was built to bridge the gap between high-performance bare-metal computing and generative artificial intelligence. We realized that traditional cloud hosting simply wasn't fast enough for real-time AI inference.

Today, we operate over 150+ private enterprise datacenters globally. We don't just host your code; our generative algorithms actively write, optimize, and distribute it directly to edge nodes around the world.

Enterprise Security (KMS)

Security is not an add-on; it is the foundation of our entire architecture. We utilize advanced End-to-End Key Management Systems (KMS) to ensure that your proprietary models and fine-tuning datasets are cryptographically isolated.

  • Data at rest: AES-256 encryption across all Vector DBs and object stores.
  • Data in transit: Strict TLS 1.3 protocol enforcement.
  • Air-gapped clusters: Diamond tier subscribers benefit from physically isolated GPU racks.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026.
